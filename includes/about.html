WHAT?

  • RompЯ is all about discovery. Discovering more about the music you love and discovering music you don't know.

WHY?

  • The collectioniser aims to do what Amarok 1.4 used to do with my music collection.
  • The looks aim to do what Amarok 2 does with my music collection. It's an MPD and Mopidy client because my media center is over there.
  • Why is it called RompЯ? It just is. I think it was going to be an acronym but I gave up at 'R' and then I liked the idea of using a 'Я' at the end.

WHERE?

  • RompЯ is developed on Manjaro KDE ARM Linux running on a Raspberry Pi 4B, and on an iMac running macOS Mojave.
  • My music system runs on a Raspberry Pi 3B+ using Raspberry Pi OS Bullseye, with Mopidy and Snapcast feeding multiple Raspberry Pis with Allo BOSS DAC sound cards (amazing things).
